clickable — wrap the badge in <a> or <button>, don't restyle the span

Badges are non-interactive by default. To make one clickable (a filter tag, a category link), wrap it in the appropriate interactive element. The badge styling stays unchanged; the wrapper provides the interaction and focus behaviour.
